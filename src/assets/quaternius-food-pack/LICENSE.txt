50 low-poly food/drink glTF models (fruits, veggies, burgers, drinks, desserts,
utensils...). "Bottle.glb" and "Bottle-Pc8dM9Ja4V.glb" are two distinct bottle
shapes (different file sizes, not duplicates). Grabbed primarily for a real beer
bottle model (see src/weapons.js), but broad enough to cover future food/drink
pickups too (apéro snacks, healing items — see GitHub issues #3/#6 brainstorm).
